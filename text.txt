Many individuals face significant financial challenges due to the rising costs of medical care, hospital bills, and prescription medications. The burden of these expenses often strains their financial well-being. Inadequate health insurance coverage exacerbates the situation, leaving individuals to bear the brunt of high treatment costs on their own. Those grappling with chronic illnesses and disabilities encounter additional financial hurdles as they require ongoing medical care and specialized treatments. The financial strain imposed by healthcare expenses can also impact mental health, as individuals face the daunting task of balancing their well-being with the cost of necessary treatments and medications. It is crucial to address these financial obstacles and explore avenues for affordable healthcare options to alleviate the burden on individuals and ensure their overall well-being.
